About Sri Sai Path Labs

Established in 1998, Sri Sai Path Labs has grown to become one of Karnataka's most trusted diagnostic centers. With over two decades of excellence in pathology services, we have consistently maintained the highest standards of accuracy, reliability, and patient care.

Our state-of-the-art laboratory is equipped with the latest diagnostic equipment from leading manufacturers including Roche, Siemens, and Abbott. We process over 500,000 samples annually with a team of experienced pathologists, microbiologists, and laboratory technicians.

We are proud to be ISO 15189:2022 certified and NABL accredited, ensuring that every test result meets international quality standards. Our commitment to continuous improvement and adoption of cutting-edge technology has made us the preferred choice for patients, doctors, and healthcare institutions.
